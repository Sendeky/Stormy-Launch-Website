  • Basics

  • How do I update the weather?

    Simply pull down the element that you want to update. This is the side element on the home page and the forecast list on the forecast page.

  • How do I add widgets?

    Exit the app and long press on your homescreen. Click the "plus" button that appears and add any desired widgets from our app.

  • Widgets aren't updating!

    If the widgets aren't updating then simply enter the app and refresh the weather. If that doesn't work then close the app completely and re-open it.
